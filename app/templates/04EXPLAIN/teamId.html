In fact it's just a number that identifies your FPL team. When you go to your team on 'https://fantasy.premierleague.com' Then click on 'points'. Now you can see your 'team id' in the address bar. For me it's 'https://fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/993831/event/1'. So my 'team id' = 993831.
